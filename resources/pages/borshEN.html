Кулинары предупреждают: борщ не прощает ошибок

Borsch is considered one of the main dishes of different nations, and has many cooking recipes. But      culinary specialists      We are sure that in any case, mistakes are unforgivable during its preparation, MIR 24 reports.

Borsch - vegetable soup with meat or mushrooms, the main first course of Ukrainian cuisine. It has gained wide international distribution and recognition.          Borsch along with dumplings is to some extent a symbol of Ukrainian national cuisine.
