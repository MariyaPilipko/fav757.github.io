About the benefits of rose hips and where to buy it

The healing properties of rose hips were appreciated in antiquity, the medieval scientist and doctor Avicenna wrote about its benefits in diseases of the liver and stomach, and in Russia in the XII century in the monastery and princely gardens grown "serberina", or "Silvergrain": that was the name of the rosehip. Rate the scope: in 1668 for the gardens of Tsar Alexei Mikhailovich there was "Sent from Murom to Moscow ... at the supply of 1,500 silver-silver trees." In the XXI century, dried berries and rose hips can be bought at any pharmacy. And what do we know today about the properties of this plants so appreciated by our ancestors?
