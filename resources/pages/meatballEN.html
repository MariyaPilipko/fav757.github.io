IKEA has revealed the secret of cooking its branded meatballs

In addition to functional and minimalist furniture, IKEA is famous for its branded meatballs, the recipe of which the company      shared online (thanks to quarantine, by the way).

Ingredients In four servings.        For matches you will need:        500 g of ground beef;        250 g of minced pork;        1 onion (finely chop);        1 clove of garlic (mash or chop);        100 g of breadcrumbs;        1 egg;        5 st. l. fat milk;        A generous pinch of salt and pepper.        For the cream sauce you will need:        A little vegetable oil;        40 g of butter;        150 ml of vegetable broth;        150 ml of beef broth;        150 ml of heavy cream;        2 tsp. soy sauce;        1 tsp. Dijon mustard.

Preparation       We prepare meatballs:       Mix ground beef and pork by hand. Add onion, garlic, breadcrumbs, egg and mix well.       Add milk and spices.       From the resulting mass make small balls. Put them on a clean plate, cover and leave in the refrigerator for 2       hours (this will help the meatballs keep their shape during cooking).       Heat the oil in a frying pan over medium heat. As soon as the pan heats up, lay out the meatballs,       fry them on all sides (until brown).       Then place the meatballs on a baking sheet and cover. Place the meatballs in a preheated oven (up to 180 ° C in normal       mode and up to 160 ° C - in convection) for 30 minutes.       Prepare cream sauce:       Melt 40 g of butter in a frying pan. Then add 40 g of wheat flour to the oil and stir       for 2 minutes. Add 150 ml of vegetable broth and 150 ml of beef broth and continue to stir.       Add 150 ml of cream, 2 tsp. soy sauce 1 tsp. Dijon mustard. Bring the mass to a boil and       wait until the sauce thickens.       Serve meatballs with potatoes. Enjoy!
